**Chosen Company for Investment:**

**Shengyi Electronics**
- **Reason for Selection:** Shengyi Electronics has shown extraordinarily impressive financial health, with its earnings increasing by a remarkable 603.3% over the past year. The company's revenue has also doubled from CNY 4.69 billion to CNY 9.49 billion, indicating strong growth potential. Analysts forecast a 44% annualized growth through the end of 2026, suggesting that the company is well-positioned to capture emerging market dynamics. These indicators make Shengyi Electronics a promising candidate for investment.

**Companies Not Selected:**

**Hacksaw**
- **Reason for Not Selecting:** While Hacksaw exhibits strong quarterly performance and has robust growth forecasts with a projected revenue growth of 30%, it does not demonstrate the same level of exceptional financial health and earnings increase as Shengyi Electronics. Therefore, despite its promising investment potential, it was not selected.

**Delton Technology (Guangzhou)**
- **Reason for Not Selecting:** Delton Technology has shown significant revenue growth and has made strides with AI demands and capital from a Hong Kong listing. However, some concerns about future profitability due to an accrual ratio of 0.50 make it a less desirable choice for investment. The potential risks associated with profitability outweighed their growth in this decision.
